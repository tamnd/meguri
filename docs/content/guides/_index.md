Each guide is built around a job rather than a flag: turning a URL list into a frontier, draining it, keeping it durable across restarts, reshaping the files when a partition runs hot or cold, and working out what the whole thing costs at a hundred billion URLs. They assume you have worked through the [quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).
